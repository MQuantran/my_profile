About Minh Quan

More than just a title — let’s dive deeper!

I am a mechanical engineer who thrives at the intersection of physical systems and intelligent software. My journey began with a fascination for how things move, which naturally evolved into a passion for Robotics and Autonomous Systems. I don't just want to design a machine; I want to give it the "brain" to navigate the world.

With a strong foundation in Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) and Reinforcement Learning, I focus on building systems that are as efficient as they are smart. Whether I'm optimizing internal flows in ANSYS or training an RL agent for autonomous navigation, my goal is to bridge the gap between traditional engineering and the future of automation.

I believe the best solutions come from curiosity and collaboration. I aim to contribute to impactful projects that push the boundaries of what machines can do for us.
